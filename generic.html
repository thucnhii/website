RUNNER-UP OF JCI HACKATHON 2022: HACK THE FUTURE

Hackathon 2022 - Hack The Future is a contest for programming ideas, where talented and enthusiastic students gather to create practical technology solutions to support businesses in the central provinces of Vietnam.

Given the questions of tackling the challenges faced by entrepreneurs during the recovery from COVID-19, our team focuses on addressing the needs of traditional market vendors.
COVID-19 has shifted consumer preferences towards healthier choice and a desire to minimize physical interactions, posing challenges for local sellers in traditional markets. To empower these vendors, my team built the application that not only allows sellers to advertise and update their products but also provides buyers with instant access to the information and the option for the safe doorstep delivery.

We had two weeks to research, outline and develop the plan, followed by a one-day hackathon to construct the demo and pitch. In my role, I focus on market research, formulated marketing strategies and provided suggestions for UX-UI design.

I am sincerely grateful for my incredible teammates who had sleepless nights to contribute their best to the project and be resilient to achieve the common goal. Gratitude is also extended to the JCI Vietnam, Danang organizer for facilitating our journey throughout the competition.
This award kickstarts and gives me more motivation in the path of data analyst, especially in marketing. I would like to develop a business supported by data-driven decisions.
